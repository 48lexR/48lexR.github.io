Characters

Below are our prized player characters.

Players

Mikey

Claire Steibach plays Mikey, a direct reference to Saint Michael, who was firm in his beliefs, funny, strong, and dilligent. He's no genius, but his strong suit is strength, anyways. He's an Aasimar Paladin who just hangs around for a good time.

Merlin

Hailey Stuttard plays Merlin Theodore Skog, who is amnesiac dubbed the ‘Wizard Lizard’ by those from his past. Merlin began the campaign with a great disinterest in his companions and the adventures they were sent on. However, as time went on, he found there was much more to the others that intrigued him...and unearthed parts of his past he had long forgotten. Slowly the party members and their adventures turn the brash, and frankly stupid, Merlin back to the “endearingly” (subject for debate) awkward scholar he once was. (Yay character development)

Ranto

Micajah Bullard plays Ranto, a half-elf paladin with a chaotic taste for justice. She’s usually pretty stern and focused, so she keeps her eyes on the prize no matter what, although she's not terribly good at planning. If she had a motto, it'd be: ‘Never fear, never falter, always fight.’
